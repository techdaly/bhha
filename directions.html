Blue Heron Healing Arts

564 SW 3rd St., Corvallis, OR 97333


Blue Heron Healing Arts is in downtown Corvallis, in the 3rd Street Professional Plaza at SW 3rd Street and Western Avenue.
Parking is available in our lot directly in front of the office.



Directions from Interstate Highway 5 Northbound,
coming from Eugene and other points South:

  • Take Exit 228/Highway 34 into Corvallis, about 10 miles
  • Cross bridge over Willamette River
  • Turn left onto SW 4th Street
  • Go 8 blocks South on 4th Street
  • Turn Left on SW Western Avenue
  • Turn Left into our parking lot on the corner
  • 552 SW 3rd is in the middle of the building

Alternate Route: Follow 99-W North out of Eugene

  • In Corvallis, 99-W becomes SW 3rd Street
  • Coming into south Corvallis, take the third left onto SW Western Avenue
  • (Western will be right after SW B Avenue and before SW Washington Ave.)
  • The parking lot is on the corner of Western and 3rd Street

Directions from Interstate Highway 5 Southbound,
coming from Salem and other points North:

  • Turn left onto SW 4th Street
